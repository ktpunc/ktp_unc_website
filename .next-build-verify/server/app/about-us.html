About KTP

A professional tech community built at Carolina.

KTP brings together students who care about building strong careers, stronger friendships, and a lasting network in tech.

Who We Are

The first professional technology fraternity in the country.

Kappa Theta Pi takes pride in being the first professional technology fraternity in the country, and the first co-ed professional tech organization at UNC-Chapel Hill. Our mission is to enhance the professional, social, and technical development of our members.

Our members come from all around campus — designers, analysts, computer scientists, engineers, entrepreneurs, economists, and more. What makes the KTP community strong is our shared passion for technology and our unique backgrounds meshing together as one.

Our alumni are part of an extensive and tight-knit network spread across the country, from Silicon Valley to New York, working at both startups and household names. They stay deeply involved, providing mentorship and opening doors for current members long after graduation.

Our Pillars

What we stand for.

Kappa Theta Pi offers brothers the support to be extraordinary during their time at UNC, centered around five core pillars.

Professional Development

Through events like interview training, resume building, one-on-one mentorship, and private company recruiting, KTP prepares members for success in any technology-related career. We take pride in developing the tech leaders of the future.

Alumni Connections

Our alumni are spread across the world working on cutting-edge technologies — at companies like Microsoft, Amazon, Apple, Google, and Meta, as well as startups, consulting firms, and more.

Social Growth

The people you meet in Kappa Theta Pi will go on to be some of your closest friends. We host social events throughout the semester — from formal and tailgates to retreat — where members bond and build lasting memories.

Technical Advancement

Kappa Theta Pi gives members numerous opportunities to grow their technical skills. Whether through a project team or a workshop, we make it easy for members to expand their expertise and build things that matter.

Academic Support

KTP brothers foster academic growth and excellence for one another. Our members have access to a supportive network of some of the brightest tech minds at UNC, always ready to help with classes and extracurricular challenges.
