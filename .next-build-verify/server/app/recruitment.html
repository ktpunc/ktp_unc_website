Eligibility

Who can apply when recruitment opens?

The basics are simple. KTP is open to students from across UNC who are excited about technology, community, and growth.

Any Major

Students across CS, business, stats, policy, and more are encouraged to apply.

2.5+ GPA

A minimum cumulative GPA of 2.5 is required for eligibility.

2+ Semesters Left

Applicants should have at least two semesters remaining at UNC.
